We’re your corner baked goods shop that specializes in rolls and buns. Making sweet things was once something done out of love for meals with friends on Capitol Hill. Now, we’ve transitioned into a storefront where we proudly sell freshly made wonders daily.

At Bun Bun Bake Shop, we use quality ingredients sourced from local farmers because we go beyond the standard roll. We believe in involving the community around us to give you something delightful each and every time you visit.
